🔒 Privacy Policy
Last updated: February 25, 2026
Overview

Super Pio is a Pi Network game. This policy explains how we handle your information when you play.

Information We Collect

Pi Username & UID: Collected when you sign in with Pi Network, used to save your progress and display your name on the leaderboard.

Game Progress: Levels completed, coins collected, and lives remaining are saved to provide a continuous experience.

Payment Data: Transaction IDs are processed through Pi Network's secure payment system. We do not store payment card details.

How We Use Your Information

• Save and restore your game progress across devices

• Display your username on the global leaderboard

• Process in-game purchases via Pi Network

Data Storage

Game data is stored securely using Supabase cloud database with industry-standard encryption.

Third-Party Services

Pi Network: Authentication and payments. Their privacy policy applies.

Supabase: Used for secure cloud data storage.

Your Rights

You may play as a guest without providing personal information. Guest progress is stored locally on your device only.

Contact

For questions, contact us through the Pi Network Developer Portal or Pi Browser app.
